## Style Prompt
Build a polished mobile-product promo for WorkaHoric with a dark, premium utility feel rooted in the app's existing teal palette. The canvas should feel focused, deliberate, and finance-adjacent rather than flashy: deep slate backgrounds, teal accents, soft cyan-tinted glows, warm amber moments for paydays, rounded product cards, and confident typography. Motion should feel brisk and reassuring, with layered reveals, gentle floating decor, and clean transitions that suggest control and clarity.

## Colors
- `#0B1320` background
- `#162033` surface
- `#0F766E` primary teal
- `#5EEAD4` bright accent
- `#E2E8F0` text
- `#F59E0B` payday highlight
- `#F7F9FC` light-theme background for the theme-toggle scene
- `#102A43` light-theme text for the theme-toggle scene

## Typography
- `Space Grotesk` for headlines
- `IBM Plex Sans` for interface text and supporting copy

## What NOT to Do
- Do not use purple or blue-neon gradients.
- Do not make the promo look like a generic SaaS dashboard ad.
- Do not rely on flat black or pure white backgrounds.
- Do not center every element with equal visual weight.
- Do not show the theme switch as a throwaway detail; make it an intentional beat.
